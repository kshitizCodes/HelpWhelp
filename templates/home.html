INTRODUCTION

What is HelpWhelp?

HelpWhelp is a web-based application that is designed with the goal to provide awareness of pet adoption. HelpWhelp's objective is to provide street animals care they need by acting as a platform where people can connect and post street animals and also adopt one.HelpWhelp is an absolute playform for everyone.

Why street animals?

Overburdened shelters take in millions of stray, abused and lost animals every year, and by adopting an animal, you're making room for others. Not only are you giving more animals a second chance, but the cost of your adoption goes directly towards helping those shelters better care for the animals they take in!

Why HelpWhelp?

Digital adoption platforms enable you to assign individual step-by-step guides or walkthroughs based on various styles of learning, and take team members through all of your company procedures. This also applies to training and the approach you use to create a knowledge base of all your company information.
